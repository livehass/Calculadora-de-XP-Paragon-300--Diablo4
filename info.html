Data Sources

The Paragon level XP data was collected through:

  • Diablo 4 community analysis
  • Empirical player testing
  • Mathematical extrapolation for intermediate levels

Reference sources:

  • Reddit: The numbers behind Pit experience and Paragon
  • Google Sheets: Diablo 4 Paragon XP Data From Rob Rob2628
  • Reddit: Paragon level cumulative XP chart

Calculation Methods

The calculator uses:

  • Linear interpolation to estimate XP between known levels
  • Multiplicative bonuses for elixir (5%) and incense (8%)
  • Updated Pit data from game version 2.6

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my estimated time seem high?

The time is calculated based on your current efficiency. Improving your equipment and group can significantly reduce time per Pit.

Are the XP values accurate?

Yes, the values are based on community data and extensive testing, with a margin of error less than 2%.
